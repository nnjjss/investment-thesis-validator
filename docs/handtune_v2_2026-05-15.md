## What worked

- **`plan_evidence` "default = enable" rule**: under-fetching was the root
  cause of the SUPPORTED-blindness diagnosed in the prior session. v2's
  hard heuristics (any fundamental claim → fetch_stock; any segment /
  cash-flow / governance claim → fetch_filings) eliminate the case where
  the synthesizer had only profile + quote to work with.
- **Synthesizer "compute from income statement" guidance**: explicitly
  showing that margin / YoY / R&D-ratio are derivable from `/income_statement`
  alone (even with `/ratios` paywalled) unlocked the fundamentals lift.
- **Arithmetic-verification hard rule** (calling out the f03 NVDA mistake
  by name in the prompt): no rerun of that specific error in v2.

## What regressed

- **Hallucination rate 0.0 → 0.07**: 7 items committed to REFUTED with
  zero citations (5× contradiction, 2× edge_case). The prompt instructs
  "cite at least one evidence id", but the model overrides on items
  where its prior knowledge ("AMZN obviously has ad revenue") feels
  more authoritative than the fetched evidence. Code-level guardrail
  needed — downgrade un-cited committed verdicts to UNCERTAIN at parse
  time in `src/agent/nodes/synthesize.py`.
- **edge_case still -10pp vs baseline**: v2's commit-more bias still
  over-shoots on delisted/share-class items where UNCERTAIN was the
  intended answer. Needs sharper guidance about un-evaluable tickers.

## What did not move

- **Sentiment 0.300** unchanged — entirely gated by `NEWS_API_KEY`
  being unconfigured. Every sentiment item gets `fetch_news` skipped,
  so the synthesizer correctly returns UNCERTAIN regardless of prompt
  tweaks. Wiring the key in is the single highest-leverage next change.
